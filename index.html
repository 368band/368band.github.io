    HOW TO PLAY SLIPSTREAM

    • Mic Permissions: When your browser asks, you MUST click "Allow" to use the microphone.
    • Setup: Select your specific instrument from the dropdown menu. Use "Tuner Mode" to check your slide/mouthpiece before racing!
    • Mic Sensitivity: If your engine won't start, click SETTINGS and lower the Mic Sens. If traffic spawns when you aren't playing, raise it!
    • Target Note: Use the < and > buttons to choose the note you want to practice.
    • Difficulty: Use the < and > buttons to change your allowed tuning range. Higher difficulty = more points!
    • Ignition: Play a steady note into the mic to start your engine. Your air is the fuel!
